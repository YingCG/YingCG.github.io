The tallest tree in New Zealand

Tallest Overall:

Australian Mountain Ash (Eucalyptus regnans) -- about 82.25 m high at Orokonui Ecosanctuary, near Dunedin.

Tallest Native

A Kahikatea (Dacrycarpus dacrydioides) at Kaniwhaniwha, around 66.5m.

Tallest Native

Tāne Mahuta ('Lord of the Forest') are New Zealand's largest by volume, but not the tallest in height. Locate at Waipoua Forest of Northland Region. Trunk girth: 13.77 m Trunk height: 17.68 m Total height: 51.2 m Trunk volume: 244.5 m³
